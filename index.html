STORY

During the Great Dragon War, Draven and Seraphine, young dragon siblings, find themselves trapped in a dungeon. The last thing they remember is their mother’s final words: 'Escape the dungeon to be safe from the war.

Characters

Draven
Draven is powerful and fearless, excelling in offense, with a daring nature that drives him into battles head-on.
Seraphine
Seraphine is strategic and supportive, often acting as the brains behind their plans, with abilities focused on protection .
